How to Use CAMPAINT

  • Point any finger up (index, middle, ring, or pinky) to paint with your selected color
  • Use multiple fingers at once to draw with multiple points!
  • Make a fist and hover over the 🖌️ Paint button (right side) for 1 second to switch to paint mode
  • Make a fist and hover over the 🧽 Erase button (right side) for 1 second to switch to erase mode
  • Make a fist and hover over the color button (top right) for 1 second to open the color picker
  • In color picker: Point your finger at the color wheel to select a color, and at the brightness slider to adjust brightness
  • Make a fist and hover over the "Done" button for 1 second to close the color picker and continue painting
  • Move your fingers around to draw amazing art!
  • Click "Show My Painting" to see your artwork on a white canvas
  • Click "Start New Painting" to clear and start fresh, or "Edit Painting" to continue!
  • Click "💾 Save as JPG" to download your masterpiece!
  • In fullscreen: Make a fist and hover over the X button (top left) for 1 second to exit
